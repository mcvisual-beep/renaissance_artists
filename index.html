National Boys and Girls Enrollments per Class

This map shows the number of students attending class from grades 1-8 by county. The information was pulled from 2014 data. The purple circles indicate boys and the orange circles indicate girls. Hovering over the circles will provide the number of students in attendence with a graph showing how students tend to progress through the grades in that region. To change the grades being examined, simply move the slider in the bottom left hand corner.
